Have Fun in San Diego

Jul 1st, 2011 | Comments

As everyone gears up for the exodus to San Diego, I wish everyone a good, enjoyable and fruitful time. There’s something to be said for the energy of 12,000+ geonerds in one place. As for me, I’ll be here, hanging with a couple hundred of the bravest, most inspiring young people you could ever meet. After that, it’s family time and and little R&R.

And be sure to use the Unofficial Esri UC BackChannel by the guys at DTS Agile. It’s a great way to enrich your experience.

Have fun and, hopefully, I’ll catch up with many of you at FOSS4G.
