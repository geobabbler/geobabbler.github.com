Dates Set for WhereCampTampaBay

Nov 21st, 2011 | Comments

I just got word from Anthony Quartararo that the dates for WhereCampTampaBay have been set for February 10-11, 2012. That seems like a pretty sweet time of year for a weekend in Florida.

By February, you’ll be ready for this.

The 10th will feature an IgniteSpatial lightning talk session with the actual unconference happening on the 11th. I had a great time at WhereCampDC back in May and I know Florida has a very active spatial community that deals with a diverse problem set. I think this has the potential to very a very good event.

To register for IgniteSpatial: http://wherecamptbignite-auto.eventbrite.com/ To register for WhereCampTampaBay: http://wherecamptbunconference-auto.eventbrite.com/
